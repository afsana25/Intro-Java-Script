Summary of the Visualization:

The visualization is created based on NYC 5 boroughs' poverty rate in 2021 from US census data. The choropleth map presents that Bronx has highest provery rate, and Manhattan has lowest Proverty Rate. The visualization created via JavaScript, leaflet.js, html, and css. The tooltip shows the poverty rate and median house Household income hovering over each boroughs, and the legend shows the poverty of rate based on the intensity of the colors.
